How It Works

Get started in just a few simple steps

1

Open the App

Visit the Photo Repository app. No signup required to get started with local storage.

2

Upload Photos

Drag and drop your photos or click the upload button. Watch them appear instantly in your gallery.

3

Browse & Enjoy

Click any photo to view it in full detail. Your gallery is organized and ready to admire.

4

Share (Optional)

Connect to a backend service to make your photos visible to others. Completely optional.
